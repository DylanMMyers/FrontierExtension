roadmap for 689 fe impl:
1. Set up env
2. Factors of the FE algorithm:
	- Formation of graph (must be input friendly with common formats)
	- Computing optimal LP for some lam_0
	- Computing ACR for lam_0 for l,r endpoints using two more LPs
	- Iterate until we define our initial range of lams
	- Rounding of returned family (also something to play around with? check paper)*********
3. Pruning lams to make a smaller subset
4. Messing around with paramaters/constraints of LPs (specifically the first bullet of step 2)
5. Good documentation + compartmentalization (end goal is all parts should be able to be stripped and be used else where)
6. Any observations, also good to interface it and make it work with visuals
